Rainbow Summer Chevron Stripes Pride Home Decor Light Switch Plate and Outlet Cover Set - MADE TO ORDER

From ModernSwitch

Modern Zig Zag Chevron light switch cover and matching outlet set. This switchplate is handcrafted using fabric from the Remix line in Summer by Robert Kaufman if your looking to coordinate with a specific look.

This set is made when you purchase it, it can take up to 2 weeks for me to complete it and mail it out. You receive 1 light switch cover, and 1 outlet.

Each light switch cover is handcrafted from a standard plastic switch plate cover and high quality designer fabric. It is then sealed so you can easily clean it. Pattern placement may vary. Screws are included
